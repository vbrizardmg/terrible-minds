How to play

Each player is dealt 5 cards and the remaining cards are placed in the center.

The first player chooses 1 card form his hand or mixes 2 cards to create a question made of 2 sentences and asks any player.

The targeted player then has to answer the question and wins the point from the card containing his answer, not matter his answer.

The player keeps this card to cumulate his points.

BUT!

At any moment, a player can play an action card. Those cards bring a little more action into the game.

OR

You can try to double the points you made. Be careful then, because someone could steal some of your points or even blow half of them.
